ABOUT ONLINE STORE

Our online store contains reagents and consumables for elemental analyzers of all manufacturers: Leco, Eltra, Bruker, Elementar, Perkin Elmer, Thermo, HORIBA, Shimadzu, EuroVector, Costech, NC Technologies and many others, including reactors, columns, tin, silver, aluminum and nickel capsules, boats, ceramic crucibles, accelerators (tungsten, iron, tin, copper and others), absorbers (quartz wool, anhydrone, cellulose, copper oxide, sodium hydroxide and others), etc.
